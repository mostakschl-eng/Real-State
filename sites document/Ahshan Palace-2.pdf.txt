Avenue Constructions Limited will make a allotment of all 
apartment of “AVENUE AHSAN PALACE” a modern and 
luxurious apartment complex, consisting of the self-contained  
apartments, reserved car parking spaces, other features as 
described in this brochure, subject to the following terms and 
conditions.
Allotment : 
Applications for allotment of apartment should be made by 
the interested buyers on the prescribed application form duly 
signed by the applicant along with the Booking Money should 
be payable at least 30% of total flat value. Avenue 
Constructions Limited has the right to accept or reject any 
application without assigning any reason there to. On 
acceptance of an application the company will issue an 
allotment letter to the applicant on which the applicant/allottee 
shall start payments as per Schedule of Payments to be 
provided along with the allotment letter. Booking money will 
be refunded against non-accepted applications with 7 
(Seven) days from the date of deposit of the same. 
Payments :
All payments should be made in favor of Avenue 
Constructions Limited In the from of demand draft of pay 
order issued schedule Bank of Bangladesh, or by crossed 
cheque of any schedule Bank of Bangladesh. Bangladeshis 
residing abroad may remit payments in foreign exchange by 
telegraphic transfer or by demand drapt as per exchange rate 
issued by Bangladesh Bank as on the date of application and 
subsequently as on the date of installments due. 
Delay in Payments :
Payment of Installments, car park and all other charges are to 
be made on due dates. The company may issue reminders to 
the allottee but not with standing the issue of reminders. The 
allottee liable to pay a charge of 20% per 60 days on the 
amount of payment delayed. 
Cancellation of Allotment :
For Delays in payment beyond 60 days the company shall  
have the right to cancel the allotment. In such an event the 
amount paid by the allottee will be refunded after again sold 
the flat to other buyers and after deducting the 10% money of 
total flat Value. 
Deed of Agreement :
Immediately after down payment, Avenue Constructions 
Limited and the allotte shall execute and agreement for 
safeguarding the interest of the allottee as well as the 
company.   
Possession & Grand Transfer :
Possession and title of  the apartment shall with Avenue 
Constructions Limited until payment of the installments and 
all other charges/dues are made in full. Allotee will own and 
undivided and un- demarcated proportionate share of the 
land. The proportionate land will be-registered in the name of 
the allottee at the cost of the allottee. 
Allotment Transfer : 
Until full payment of all installments/dues are made of Avenue 
Constructions Limited and before the final and formal handing 
over of the possessions to the Allottee, The allottee shall not 
have any right to transfer the allotment to a third party. In case 
of any transfer to any third party after completion of the 
project, allottee will be charge for a transfer fees of Tk. 
2,00,000/- with the application for transfer to a third party. In 
that case all the expenditure related to the documentation 
made process according to the land authority’s Rules and 
Regulations shall also be borne by the allottee with incidental 
charge.
Co-operative Society :
In the intent of all the occupants of the apartment complex, 
Avenue Constructions Limited “Apartment Owner’s Association” 
will be formed. Each allotted, after payment of all installments 
will be a member of the Association.
Association Fee 50,000/- (Fifty Thousand Taka Only)
Service Facilities Cost :
Connection fees, security deposits and other incidental charges 
and costs related to gas, water, sewerage and electric 
connections are to be paid by the allottee should be payable 
4,00,000/-
Documentation cost :
The allottee shall bear costs related to transfer/registration of 
the apartment and the undivided un-demarketed proportionate 
share of land. Income Tax (IT), Value added tax (VAT) and 
other any cost (if any) as charged by the Government shall be 
borne by the allottee with incidental charge.
Changes :
Minor Changes may be incorporated by Avenue Constructions 
Limited in design and specification should these become 
necessary. Allottee shall also have the option to make minor 
internal change within the possibilities and  limitations of total 
building system. There is no option change any outer elevation. 
Line Schedule Of Construction :
Construction of Avenue Constructions Limited  is Scheduled 
to be  Started on April, 2026 the time schedule is methodically 
prepared to ensure both high quality and smooth progress of 
work. Work is scheduled to be completed within 24 months 
from the date of commencement of work. The time schedule 
may only be extended by a reasonable time limit due to 
unforeseen circumstances which are beyond the control of 
the company.
